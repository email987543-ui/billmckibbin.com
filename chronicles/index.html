The Series

Chronicles of the Still Lake

The water is calm. That is what should frighten you.

The Still Lake is the source of every gift in the world — and its surface is time itself. Draw from it and the water ripples, and ripples run through what was and what will be. The deeper the draw, the farther they travel.

Some are born with the shape for it. Fewer ever learn the difference between receiving what the water offers and reaching for what it doesn't. The Chronicles are about that difference — told quietly, at the level of hands and breath and choices, in a world still living inside the consequences of people who reached.

The magic keeps its secrets. So does the Lake. Part of the story is earning them.
