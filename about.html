The Author

Bill McKibbin

Bill McKibbin is the author of Chronicles of the Still Lake, a new epic fantasy series beginning December 2026 with Book One and the free prequel novella Stories From The Still Lake.

He takes his cues on fantasy and world-building from the authors who inspired him — Robert Jordan, Brandon Sanderson, Terry Brooks, Terry Goodkind, and many others. Chronicles of the Still Lake is a twelve-book series spanning three thousand years: the world before the cataclysm called the Surge, and the world forever changed by it.

The first trilogy releases across a single year, each book finished before the one before it launches — progress is public on the progress page.

The mailing list is the best way to reach him — and the first thing it sends you is free.
